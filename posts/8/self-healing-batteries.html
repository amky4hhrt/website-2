'Brain Batteries' Inspired by Electric eels



Some researchers at the 'University of Cambridge' have came of with a special type of battery which have both self healing property and strechability.

These batteries can be used in the wearables and bio-implants but it can also be used to treat some neurological conditions.

These batteries are inspired by an underwater creature called the Electric eel.
